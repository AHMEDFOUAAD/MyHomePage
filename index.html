About Me

Seasoned Clinical Pharmacist with a robust 20-year track record across Egypt, the UAE, and Qatar. Holder of Master's degrees in Pharmacotherapy and Clinical Research, complemented by Board certifications in Pharmacotherapy and Geriatrics, and Diplomas in Healthcare Quality and Clinical Research.

Demonstrated proficiency in delivering patient-centric care, advancing clinical research with many cited publications, and excelling in educational capacities.

Actively involved in international pharmaceutical conferences as a scientific committee member, speaker, and moderator and played a pivotal role in formulating numerous National Geriatric Guidelines in Qatar, showcasing a strong commitment to healthcare excellence and leadership in geriatric pharmacotherapy.

As a dedicated professional in the field of clinical pharmacy, I am passionate about improving patient outcomes through evidence-based medication management and interdisciplinary collaboration.

My expertise spans across various domains including geriatric pharmacotherapy, clinical research, quality improvement, and educational leadership.

I am committed to continuing my contribution to the advancement of pharmaceutical care, particularly for older adults, through research, education, and clinical practice leadership.
